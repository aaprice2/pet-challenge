Let's Talk about Pets...

Remi Rooster

Remi Rooster is thought to be a Rat Terrier, Chihuahua, Jack Russell Terrier mix. AKA he's a little mini-mutt! Remi came into our lives in April of 2022 through a local no-kill shelter. Weighing in at only 16 pounds, he's the perfect addition to our family. Remi enjoys running around at the dog park, chasing squirrels, and most of all - EATING! As long as he is with one of his paw-rents he is a happy camper. Remi is full of love and just loves to snuggle and get pets as much as possible. He is a very good boy! üê∂

Oliver the Cat

Oliver is our tuxedo kitty! Always dressed to impressed, Oliver is a very personable cat. He more often acts like a dog in a cat's body. Oliver is 7 years old. He is the spunkiest kitty cat you have ever seen. He's a healthy 13 pounds and basically bigger than Remi. Oliver loves to lounge in the sun, get pets, and most of all - get treats. Oliver also joined our family through a no-kill shelter! We knew Oliver was right for our family after about 10 seconds. Oliver is the best big boy cat for our family!üê±
